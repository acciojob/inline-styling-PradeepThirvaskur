Roses pictures

A rose is a type of shrub, which is known all over the world for its beauty. Roses come in a variety of colours such as yellow, pink, white and red. The rose is seen as a sign of harmony and balance. A red rose is a symbol of love, however, a deep red rose is a symbol of regret and sorrow.
